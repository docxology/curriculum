Researchers are moving beyond purely symbolic representations to build AI systems that directly perceive and act within physical spaces. The integration of proprioception and haptic feedback is crucial for building agents that possess a ‘sense of self’ and the ability to navigate unfamiliar terrains. Recent progress involves using generative adversarial networks (GANs) to create realistic synthetic environments for training robots. The development of robust state estimation algorithms—combining sensor data with predictive models— is critical to this area.
