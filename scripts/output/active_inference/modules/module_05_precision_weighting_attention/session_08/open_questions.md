## Open Question 2: How does Federated Learning Affect Data Privacy?

Context: Federated learning (FL) offers a novel approach to machine learning where models are trained directly on decentralized devices (e.g., smartphones) rather than sending data to a central server. This drastically reduces the risk of data breaches and maintains user privacy. However, the effectiveness of FL’s privacy guarantees is complex and depends on various factors. Current research: Investigates the impact of techniques like differential privacy, secure aggregation, and homomorphic encryption to further enhance privacy protection within FL systems.
